# Reference style (photorealistic coast)

Match `reference_coast.png`: **aerial drone photograph**, not illustration or texture swatch.

## Sea

- Deep navy offshore grading to clear turquoise near shore
- Visible rocky seabed in shallows; fine surface ripples
- **No** flat cyan fill, **no** cartoon waves

## Land (dry scrubland)

- Granular sandy/pebbly ground, sun-bleached ochre and grey stone
- Sparse rounded shrubs / olive scrub with soft shadows (light from NW)
- Small stone ruin acceptable; no bright yellow borders

## Shores

- Rocky boulder beach, natural irregular edge
- Land and water meet organically — not a straight color block

## Critical — tiling

- Paint **edge-to-edge**; no picture frame, no border, no vignette
- Outer rim must continue the same terrain/water as the interior (seam-friendly)
- Do **not** leave blank, white, or solid-color margins
