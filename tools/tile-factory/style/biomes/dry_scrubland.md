# Biome: dry_scrubland — Dry rocky scrubland (v1)

**Season v1:** summer

## Palette

- Sea: deep navy → clear turquoise shallows over pale rock
- Land: sun-bleached ochre, grey stone, olive-green shrubs

## Features

- Rocky boulder coast (not flat sand bars)
- Sparse rounded shrubs; occasional small pine cluster (2–5 trees max)
- Granular pebbly ground texture — avoid flat yellow dirt fills
- Optional: small stone ruin, subtle terracing on land-only tiles

## Shores

- Irregular natural coastline; white foam only as thin surf line, not a thick band
