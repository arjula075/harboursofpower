# Biome: sparse_olive — Sparse olive-like trees (v1)

**Season v1:** summer

## Palette

- Sea: deep navy → clear turquoise shallows over pale rock and sand
- Land: warm tan soil, pale limestone, silver-green olive foliage
- Beach: sun-bleached sand with light pebbles

## Features

- Scattered olive trees (Olea-style): silvery-green rounded canopies, short trunks, irregular spacing — not dense forest
- Understory: dry grass tufts, low herbs, exposed pale soil and stone between trees
- Rocky natural coast with occasional low scrub near the shore
- Avoid: dark conifer forest, lush lawn, cartoon trees, thick impassable woodland

## Shores

- Irregular natural coastline; thin white surf only
- Shallow turquoise band near coast, uniform deep blue at open-sea tile edges
- Cardinal shores: coast meanders between opposite edge midpoints; silhouette irregularity required
- Diagonal shores: small sea triangle in corner

## Variation

- v01–v03: identical coast geometry; move individual olive trees and ground patches only
