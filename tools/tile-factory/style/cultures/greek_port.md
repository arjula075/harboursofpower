# Port overlay: Greek (v1)

- **Type:** 512×512 overlay on land or shore base cell
- **Era:** classical / Hellenistic Mediterranean (not modern)

## Must include

- Whitewashed or pale plaster buildings with terracotta roofs
- Small temple or stoa silhouette, agora-scale (not megacity)
- One or two stone quays / breakwater hints if on coast
- Optional: single trireme or fishing boat **small** at dock (not dominant)

## Avoid

- Roman triumphal arches as focal point, Egyptian pylons, medieval castles
- Text, banners with readable letters

## Readability

- Recognizable as Greek at 128px preview
- Center-weighted composition; soft/feathered blend into terrain at outer 10% if needed
